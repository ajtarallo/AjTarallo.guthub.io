Boston has a team in all four of the major sport leagues.

  • Patriots (NFL
  • Red sox (MLB)
  • Celtics (NBA)
  • Bruins (NHL)
Boston sports are very special to the people of Boston, the teams represent the city and its people. Often the teams take on the personality of the people who live in the city. Boston sports are often good and don't struggle to fill the seats.
