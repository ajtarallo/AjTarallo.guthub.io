Boston is the capital of Massachusetts. Boston is a busy city with lots to do. The city contains a wide variety of food options including Faneuil hall which is an attraction itself. If you are visiting you will not struggle to find something to do.

Boston fun facts

  • Many of the cities first settlers came from Boston, England and simply kept the name.
  • The first dunkin donuts was created in Quincy a town just outside of Boston
  • The Boston university bridge is the only one in the world where a boat can sail under a train going under a vehicle driving under an airplane.
